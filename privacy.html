First Party Data Collection

All requests to this website, with the exception of photos.thistlebunny.com, are logged by the webserver. These logs contain the timestamp, IP address, user agent, and request URI for each request.

Logs recorded by the webserver are retained for 14 days and then automatically deleted. These logs are used for standard operational and troubleshooting only, and are not shared with third parties.
